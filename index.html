I am a software engineer with a passion for problem-solving, with practical experience in using a variety of technologies, including JavaScript, Python, React, Redux, SQL, Flask, Sequelize, REST APIs, HTML, CSS, TDD methodologies, and have a passing acquaintance with C#, C++, Java, and PHP. As a newish resident to the Bay-Area, I am interested to explore the region and see what it has to offer. I am hoping to, with my work, improve the well being of others, and I believe that programming can play an important part in accomplishing that goal. I am looking to help people find elegant solutions to complex problems, while ever learning new skills to improve my ever expanding repertoire.
